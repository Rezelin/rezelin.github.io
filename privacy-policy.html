Privacy Policy for Space Cat Arcade Adventure

Last Updated: July 07, 2025

Welcome to Space Cat Arcade Adventure! This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our game. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency.

Information Collection and Use

We do not collect, store, or process any personal data from users for any purpose beyond what is necessary to provide the game. We do not use your data for non-game-related activities.

  • No Personal Data Collection: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personally identifiable information.
  • AdMob Usage: Our game may use AdMob for advertisements. AdMob may collect certain non-personal data (e.g., device identifiers or ad interaction data) as part of its ad delivery service. This data is managed by Google in accordance with their Privacy Policy. We do not have access to or control over this data.
  • No Other Data Use: We do not track your location, behavior, or other sensitive information outside of what AdMob requires.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer any user data to third parties, except as required for AdMob ad functionality (managed by Google). We do not disclose your information to any other entities.

Children's Privacy

Our game is intended for general audiences, including children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If AdMob collects any data, it is handled under Google's Family Center policies, and we encourage parents to review Google's privacy settings.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted here with an updated date. We recommend checking back for updates.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at goblinshamangames@gmail.com.
